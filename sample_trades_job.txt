Job Title: Journeyman Electrician

Company: ProBuild Construction Services
Location: Miami, FL
Type: Full-Time
Pay Range: $28-$35/hour based on experience

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Journeyman Electrician to join our growing team. The ideal candidate will have strong commercial and residential electrical experience, with a focus on new construction and renovation projects.

Key Responsibilities:
- Install, maintain, and repair electrical wiring, equipment, and fixtures
- Ensure all work complies with relevant codes (NEC, state, and local)
- Read and interpret blueprints, technical diagrams, and specifications
- Install circuit breakers, transformers, and other electrical components
- Perform routine maintenance on electrical systems
- Troubleshoot electrical issues using testing devices
- Collaborate with general contractors and other trades on job sites
- Maintain safe working environment and follow OSHA guidelines

Required Qualifications:
- Valid Florida Journeyman Electrician License
- Minimum 4 years of electrical experience
- OSHA 30 certification required
- Strong knowledge of NEC and local electrical codes
- Experience with both commercial (208V/480V) and residential (120V/240V) systems
- Ability to read and interpret electrical blueprints
- Physical ability to lift up to 50 lbs and work in various conditions
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
- Must own basic hand tools

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with hurricane impact installations
- Knowledge of solar panel installation
- Experience with generator installation and transfer switches
- Arc flash safety training
- Bilingual (English/Spanish) is a plus

Benefits:
- Competitive hourly rate based on experience
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) with company match
- Paid time off and holidays
- Company vehicle for foremen positions
- Tool allowance program
- Continuing education opportunities

Safety Requirements:
- Must maintain clean driving record
- Pass pre-employment drug screening
- Commitment to zero-accident workplace
- Proper PPE usage at all times

To Apply:
Submit your resume highlighting your electrical experience, licenses, and certifications. Include references from recent electrical projects.
